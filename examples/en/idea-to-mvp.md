# Idea to MVP (without chaos)

## Situation
"I have a startup idea, but I do not know where to begin".

## Unstructured ask
"Give me a full plan for business, marketing, tech, and sales".

## Structured ask
"Help me define an MVP in one week. I have part-time availability and no hiring budget. Give me only 3 steps in order, each with a success check".

## Expected output
- Step 1: define the problem and primary user.
- Step 2: design the smallest version that proves value.
- Step 3: test with 5 real users.

## Takeaway
Validate value first, then scale complexity.
